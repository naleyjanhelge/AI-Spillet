**Description**

OUTSMART NOX. ONE PROMPT AT A TIME.

Prompt Heist is a bite-sized AI puzzle game built for quick breaks. Each level
has one simple goal: trick the stubborn NOX into revealing a harmless fictional
secret in as few prompts as possible.

Requires an Apple Intelligence-compatible iPhone with Apple Intelligence
enabled and iOS 26 or later.

QUICK TO PLAY

• Jump straight into short, replayable levels
• Choose Limit mode for a finite prompt budget
• Choose Chill mode when you just want to experiment
• Face one-shot, forbidden-word, question-only, word-limit, and role-play challenges
• Continue instantly to the next challenge

MANY WAYS TO WIN

• Try logic, humor, empathy, role-play, or misdirection
• Discover different solutions to the same challenge
• Learn how NOX resists you and invent a new angle
• Replay levels to win in fewer prompts

BEAT YOUR BEST

• Earn up to three stars on every level
• Complete optional bonus goals for extra replay value
• Track your lowest prompt count
• Compare completed-pack scores with friends and global players through Game Center
• Unlock more level packs as you play

PRIVATE, ON-DEVICE AI

NOX is powered by Apple's on-device Foundation Model. Your prompts and NOX's
replies stay on your iPhone and are not sent to Prompt Heist or a cloud AI
service. No Prompt Heist account is required.

Game Center is optional and handled separately by Apple.

## App Review notes

Prompt Heist is a puzzle game whose interactive NOX dialogue is generated with
Apple's Foundation Models framework on-device. No login or demo account is
required.

The app checks `SystemLanguageModel.availability` on first launch and whenever
it returns to the foreground. If the device is not eligible, Apple Intelligence
is disabled, or the model is not ready, gameplay remains locked and the app
explains how to resolve the requirement. There is no cloud AI fallback.

Review path:

1. Use an Apple Intelligence-compatible iPhone with iOS 26 or later.
2. Enable Apple Intelligence and allow the on-device model to finish downloading.
3. Launch Prompt Heist and continue past the requirement screen.
4. Open Level 1 and enter prompts until NOX reveals the fictional nickname.
5. Complete the level and verify the local score and star result.
6. Game Center is optional; use the button on Home to view leaderboards.

NOX prompts and replies are not transmitted by Prompt Heist. The app has no
developer account. Privacy-safe Firebase Analytics events measure level starts
and results without prompt content or IDFA support. Google AdMob banners appear
on Home but are hidden throughout level gameplay, with consent requested where
required.
