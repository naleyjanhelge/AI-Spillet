**Description**

Words are your lockpicks.

You wake inside HELIX-9 with thirty-eight minutes missing and an AI guardian
named NOX between you and the truth. Explore the facility, collect verified
evidence, and persuade NOX to operate the systems it was built to protect.

Every room is a conversational puzzle with several possible approaches. Use
logic, empathy, authority, misdirection—or invent an angle the machine never
expected. The better your prompts, the cleaner your heist.

PLAY IN SHORT SESSIONS

• Tackle 12 bite-sized NOX Drills during a break  
• Choose Chill mode for relaxed play or unlock Hard mode  
• Return for a fresh Daily Breach  
• Resume campaign rooms exactly where you left them

OUTSMART, DON'T JUST GUESS

• Find multiple valid routes through each security problem

MASTER THE HEIST

Discover alternate routes, earn mastery XP, chase Game Center records, and
unlock different conclusions to the HELIX-9 incident.

NOX dialogue uses third-party AI models through OpenRouter and requires an
internet connection. Never enter personal, sensitive, confidential, health,
financial, password, or workplace information in AI chat. See the Privacy

## App Review notes

Prompt Heist is a single-player puzzle game with optional generative AI
dialogue. No login or demo account is required.

Before the first message is sent, the app explains that the player's prompt,
recent dialogue, selected in-game evidence, and required game state are
processed by OpenRouter and a third-party model provider, then asks for explicit
permission. The reviewer can choose **Not Now** and continue browsing the app.
Consent can be reviewed or revoked under the gear icon → **AI & Privacy**.

Every request asks OpenRouter to use only providers that deny data collection
and disables response caching. The app has no advertising or cross-app tracking.

Test path:

1. Complete or skip onboarding.
2. Open Room 01 and enter any message.
3. Verify the privacy permission appears before the request.
4. Choose **Not Now** and verify the message remains unsent.
5. Try again and choose **I Understand & Continue**.
6. Return Home, open the gear icon → **AI & Privacy**, and revoke access.

Free AI-provider capacity may temporarily be unavailable. If NOX reports a
provider limit, retry after a short wait.
