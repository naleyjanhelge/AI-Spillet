Need a way out?

If HELIX-9 behaves unexpectedly, send a short description of the problem, your device model, iOS version, and the Prompt Heist version shown in the App Store.

Email support

Before contacting support

  • Check that the device has a working internet connection before messaging NOX.
  • Free AI providers can briefly reach capacity. Wait a moment and retry if NOX reports a provider limit.
  • Open Settings → AI & Privacy to confirm AI access is enabled.
  • Restart the app if audio, animation, or a room state appears stuck.
Do not include secrets in a support email.

Remove passwords, financial information, private workplace data, and other personal or sensitive content. A screenshot can contain notifications or other identifying details, so check it carefully before sending.

Campaign reset

Settings → Reset campaign removes active progress, evidence, scores, and saved room dialogue from the device. This cannot be undone. AI permission is managed separately under Settings → AI & Privacy.
